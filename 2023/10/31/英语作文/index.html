小作文

开头

This is Li Ming. I am writing this letter with my full heart for the purpose of putting forward some suggestion
我是李明，我写此信的目的是为了提一些建议

As one ordinary resident living here for more than twenty years, I feel obligated to write this very letter to state my concern over the increasingly deteriorating environmental situation.
作为一个在这里生活了二十来年的普通居民，我觉得有必要写下此信来陈述我的担心，关于日益恶化的环境现状。

On behalf of the Postgraduate Association, we are formally announcing that volunteers are needed.
谨代表研究生协会，我们正式宣布，招聘志愿者

主体段

It is widely shared among local residents that White Pollution surely has a crippling effect on our living surroundings.
当地居民普遍认为，白色污染给我们生活的环境带来严重危害

It is well known that our final competition is going to be held at 7:00 pm, next Friday, in the Auditorium of our university.
众所周知，决赛将于下周五晚 7 点在我们学校礼堂举行

结尾段

Thanks a lot for what you have done for me. Your prompt reply will be highly valued.
感谢你为我所做的一切。如能尽快回复，我将不胜感激。

Thanks a lot for your attention to this letter. Hopefully, some positive changes could happen in
the foreseeable future.
感谢你对此信的关注。希望，在可以预见的未来，发生积极的变化。

Your participation is more than welcome. Please feel free to contact us via the number attached.
欢迎您的加入。请通过所附电话号码与我们联系

功能句

当我们拒绝的时候该说些什么

I once thought this job could be challenging, but it turned out (that) I was wrong.
我曾认为这个工作是有挑战的，结果表明我错了。

This morning, I had just been informed that my grandmother is seriously ill. Thus, I must go
back to see her, and I am going to leave this afternoon. 今天早上，我刚得知，我奶奶病了。因此，我必须去看她，今儿下午就走。

当我们建议的时候说些什么

I have to say bitterly that the service of our campus library is going from bad to worse with each passing year.
我不得不痛心地说，我们学校图书馆的服务，一年比一年差

At the very beginning, the department concerned is supposed to play a more positive role and shoulder its due responsibilities.
首先，有关部门应该发挥更积极的角色，并承担相应的责任

If I can be of any assistance in any way, please do not hesitate to call me.
如果我能够以任何方式提供任何帮助，请放心联系我。

你是这样的人

Thus, all the candidates should meet the requirements precisely. At the very beginning, the applicant should have the ability to use English fluently, which is the basic requirement for the position. What’s more, all the students willing to apply for the position are supposed to have relevant working experience as a volunteer. Last but not least, those undergraduates with professional knowledge on xxx are much preferable.
因此，所有的候选人都应该完全满足以下要求。首先，申请人应该熟练使用英语，这是该岗位的基本需求。此外，所有希望申请该岗位的学生，都应该有相关的当志愿者的工作经验。最后，那些有 xxx 领域专业知识的人，优先

推荐电影、书籍时候

《Harry potter》、《Moment in Peking》

The reasons for recommendation are as follows. At the very beginning, it tells us a touching story, which helps me know what really matters in our life before it is too late. What’s more, after watching it too many times, I have advanced my English ability greatly. It is widely shared that English is playing an increasingly important role in today’s world, a world undergoing major developments, transformations, and adjustments. Thus, I recommend it to you as one of the
best movies I have ever watched.
我推荐的理由如下。首先，它讲述了一个动人的故事，让我在为时未晚之前，明白什么才是生命里最重要的。此外，看过许多遍之后，我的英语能力得到了很大的提升。众所周知，英语在当今世界扮演着越来越重要的角色，当今世界正在经历着大发展大变革大调整。因此，我向你推荐它，是我看过的最好的电影之一。

I am sure you will love it as much as I do. I am looking forward to having a talk with you after you watch it.
我确定，你会如我一般喜爱它。期待你观看后，与我交流

当我们推荐旅游景点的时候，我们说些什么？

As we both know, you love China and Chinese culture very much. Thus, I would like to introduce two places for your consideration. At the very beginning, you need to go to the Great Wall, which can surely deepen your understanding of Chinese rich history and culture. Besides, it is advisable for you to pay a visit to Peking University, in that it is the most prestigious and top academic institution in China.
我们都知道，你爱中国，和中国的文化。因此，我想介绍以下两个地方供您参考。首先，你应该去一下长城，这肯定会让你加深对中国悠久历史和绚烂文化的理解。此外，还建议你参观一下北大，因为北大是中国最负盛名，的顶尖学府。

当我们投诉的时候，我们说些什么？

The product doesn’t function properly. I checked the manual several times, but I could not find a solution to this problem.
该产品不能正常工作，我查了说明手册，但是找不到解决的办法。

Hopefully, you could take my complaint seriously, and give me a prompt and favourable reply.
希望，能够认真对待我的投诉，并及时给我一个满意的答复。
